About Me.

Welcome!

I'm a User Experience Designer based in the D.C. area. Previously the lead designer for Hinge, I currently freelance and design goal-oriented, human-centric digital solutions for iOS, Android, and Web applications.

To me, UX and design are more than trying to identify the users’ pain points/frustrations or solving for X; it’s about truly understanding the users’ goals, behaviors, and motivations. Only then, will you identify the real problem.

I have experience in UX research, persona development, wireframing, prototyping, interaction design, UI design, design system development, and more. If you’d like to know more about what I do or work together, contact me! I am available for freelance, contract, and full-time opportunities.
